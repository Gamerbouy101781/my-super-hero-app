About

This website is a school project I created to showcase my favorite superhero, Thor.


I built this app to practice my skills in web development/design and to share cool information about Thor from the Marvel Universe.


Thor is one of my favorite superheroes because of his strength, his story, and how he grows as a character. I really enjoyed learning more about him while making this site.


Everything on this site—like the layout, content, and design—was part of my effort to make a fun and informative superhero app.


Thank you for checking it out!
